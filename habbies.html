KHO-KHO

"Kho-kho" is a traditional Indian sport. It is a tag game that involves running, chasing, and dodging, and it's known for its speed and agility. The game is played on a rectangular field with two teams, one chasing and one defending.

Here's a more detailed breakdown:

Traditional Sport:

Kho-kho is a , centuries-old sport in India.

Tag Game:

It's a tag-based game where players from the chasing team try to touch or "tag" members of the opposing team.

Field and Teams:

The game is played on a rectangular field with two teams 12 each. One team is the chaser, and the other is the defender.

Chasing and Defending:

In each inning, the teams alternate between chasing and defending. The chasing team tries to tag the defenders within a set time limit.

Tactics and Strategy:

Kho-kho is not just about speed; it also involves tactics and strategy players try to outmaneuver each other.

Physical Fitness:

The game is great for physical fitness, improving cardiovascular endurance, agility, and speed, according to online resources.
